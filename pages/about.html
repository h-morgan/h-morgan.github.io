About Me

I went to Worcester Polytechnic Institute (WPI) in Worcester, Massachusetts for my undergraduate degree in engineering. I've always been someone who was interested in tech and building things - but while I was in university I discovered my passion for programming, after discovering I could build things online. I have been learning, building, and developing ever since.

When I'm not working or sharpening my dev skills, I like to spend my time traveling to new places, reading, spending time with my family, and going to the beach in Cape Cod.

I'm passionate about building things, solving problems, and learning new skills. I love working on new projects and helping people develop innovative solutions to their problems. I am based out of Boston, MA, but work remotely as well.

My goal is to help non-profits and small businesses have the ability to have functional, modern, responsive websites - allowing them to obtain more widespread reach.
