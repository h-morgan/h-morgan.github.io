About Me

Thanks for visiting my site! I’m an engineer and software developer based out of Boston, MA. I graduated from Worcester Polytechnic Institute (WPI) with a Bachelor of Science degree in engineering in 2016. I currently work as a software engineer for a company called Boston Fusion, an R&D company that works on projects developing technology solutions for various Department of Defense (DoD) and intelligence agencies.

My role at Boston Fusion is to design, develop, and test software in collaboration with teams of other researchers and programmers. Most of the projects I work on involve processing and working with massive amounts of data from a variety of sources -- which has led me to develop a deep understanding of the the design, application, and implementation of a variety of machine learning and deep learning techniques. The projects at Boston Fusion require a true combination of building software and developing and applying machine learning algorithms to solve complex data-oriented problems.

In my spare time I work to expand my software development, programming, and machine learning knowledge base every day - and work to create and learn in a variety of different languages, frameworks, and tools. I'm currently working on completing a program from the University of Pennsylvania, called "Computer Science Essentials for Software Development". You can check out some of the things I’ve worked on via my Github or the Projects page of this site. Feel free to get in touch with me via the Contact Me section of this site!
