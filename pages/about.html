About Me

Thanks for visiting my site! I’m an engineer and software developer based out of Boston, MA. I'm currently working as a research programmer for Boston Fusion, an R&D company that works on research programs developing technology solutions for various Department of Defense (DoD) and intelligence agencies. I completed my undergraduate education in engineering at Worcester Polytechnic Institute (WPI) in 2016.

My role at Boston Fusion is to design, develop, and test software in collaboration with teams of other and programmers and scientists. Most of the projects at Boston Fusion involve processing and working with massive amounts of data from a variety of sources - which has given me the opportunity to be exposed to a multitude of machine learning and deep learning algorithms and techniques. Every day I continue to expand upon my understanding of the the design, application, and implementation of these techniques. The projects at Boston Fusion require a true combination of developing and applying algorithms and building software in order to solve complex data-oriented problems.

In my spare time I work to expand my software development, programming, and machine learning knowledge base every day - and work to create and learn in a variety of different languages, frameworks, and tools. I'm currently working on completing the Computer Science Essentials for Software Development certificate program from the University of Pennsylvania. You can check out some of the things I’ve worked on via my Github or the Projects page of this site. Feel free to get in touch with me via the Contact Me section of this site!
