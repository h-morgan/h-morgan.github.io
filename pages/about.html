About Me

I went to Worcester Polytechnic Institute (WPI) in Worcester, Massachusetts for my undergraduate degree in engineering. While I was in university I discovered my passion for technology, web development, and programming in general. Since graduating, I have been working on advancing my development skills through a variety of projects and coursework.

When I'm not building things or studying, I enjoy traveling, exploring new places, and being at the beach. I also love to play and watch sports, my favorites being golf (to play) and football (to watch).

I'm passionate about building things, solving problems, and learning new skills. I love working on new projects and helping people develop innovative solutions to their problems. I am based out of Boston, MA, but work remotely as well.

My goal is to help non-profits and small businesses have the ability to have functional, modern, responsive websites - allowing them to obtain more widespread reach.
