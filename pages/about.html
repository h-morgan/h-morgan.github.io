About Me

Where I work

I currently work as a senior backend software engineer for Datapeople, a startup based in New York City that provides tools around recruiting & hiring analytics, with a mission to make the hiring process more efficient and fair.

Previously, I've worked as a software engineer in both the financial investment and defense industries. I completed my undergraduate education in engineering at Worcester Polytechnic Institute (WPI) in 2016.


What I do

I specialize in building and working with data-intensive applications. I build and maintain data pipelines, 3rd party integrations, APIs, and the associated backend infrastructure.

A common theme I've noticed in my career so far is the issue of organizations needing to use or make sense of data originating from a multitude of different sources. The challenge is coming up with a solution to normalize or standardize this multi-source data, to enable uniform processing and/or use within an application. This a challenge that needs to be solved independently for each unique data source and business/use case.

Some examples of the types of projects I've worked on include:

  • Building and scaling financial data pipelines for use in investment services applications
  • Developing internal tools for organizations to better make use of their data, and enable better decision-making
  • Designing and standing up various cloud-computing solutions in both AWS and Azure
  • Designing and developing integrations with external applications to enable realtime use of 3rd party application data
  • Database and API design and development to expose access to backend data in a usable way
  • Development of tools to automate time-consuming manual internal processes

I am always building things and continuing to learn in my spare time. In 2020, I built and maintained the full stack of software tools and website for hntTax, a cryptocurrency-focused tax firm specializing in services for crypto miners. Most recently, I've been working on building a tool that retrieves company financial filings from the SEC EDGAR database, and applies NLP to summarize sections and extract keywords.
